Full description

This great sized two bedroom flat on the second floor of a period conversation includes a private balcony and separate kitchen. The flat was previously lived in by the landlord and therefore has a very homely feel to it.

Directions - Tachbrook street is perfectly located between Victoria and Pimlico stations, with the cafes and shops of Warwick Way on your doorstep. There is a daily Farmers market, in addition to Waitrose, Sainsbury and Tesco Local.
